The Role of Paan in Sanskrit Erotic Literature

An interdisciplinary exploration of tambula — the betel leaf — and its significance in the erotic poetry, dramatic works, and courtship traditions of classical Sanskrit literature. From Kalidasa's similes to Vatsyayana's prescriptions, we trace how a simple leaf became one of the most enduring symbols of desire in Indian literary history.
